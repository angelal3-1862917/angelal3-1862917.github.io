Welcome to rework

Making something everyone has into something no one has

This is reWork, a small company started by a girl who was bored of her clothes. Explore my mind through fabric alterations and what barely counts as fashion design :)

how it all started

Curious as to how I a turned an idea into a company? Here's the short version!

Getting Inspired

I saw many other creative human beings altering vintage/thrifted clothing into something more modern and interesting on social media, YouTube, etc. I used my dad's old sewing machine and decided to try it out!

Creating Designs

All of my designs come from trial and error! The medium I use to design my pieces depend on the type of piece I'm attempting to design. If my design is based on creating unique graphics on a basic article of clothing, I'll typically use a a digital program (usually Illustrator) to create my graphics. If I want to change up the actual structure of the clothing piece, I'll simply make sketches and see what works with the sewing machine!

marketing

In order to sustain my company, I needed to sell my product (obviously). I mainly started this company as a hobby, so I wasn't looking to create a large profit. However, I needed the money to continue buying materials, especially since a lot of material was used to test out designs. I marketed my company mostly through Instagram and various campaigns. I wanted to appeal to my audience by being transparent, sustainable, and unique. I also made sure to create the highest quality photos and clothing pieces that I possibly could!

Selling pieces

I started off selling my pieces by having people DM me on Instagram, but now that I have my webiste up and running, most of my sales come from here! I'm very grateful for all of my customers and I hope that they keep coming back with each new collection I release!
